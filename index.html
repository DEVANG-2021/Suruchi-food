Delivering pure joy through authentic regional and street food, ensuring flawless quality and absolute safety.

"Suruchi" originates from India and means "Great Delight" and "Happiness." Suruchi Foods aims to bring light to others' lives, reflecting the characteristics of its name. The brand focuses on providing good food with an emphasis on quality, hygiene, and taste. Launched in 2002, Suruchi Foods began with a partnership with Future Group, India's Largest Retail Chain. Starting as a small venture, by 2018, Suruchi Foods expanded to almost 60 malls across India. The brand has a presence in 17 towns, showcasing its widespread reach. Employing over 600 people by 2018, Suruchi Foods contributes to local employment. A strategic tie-up with Future Group reflects its integration into India's largest retail chain. Suruchi Foods is committed to delivering taste aligned with customer preferences. The brand's journey has transformed it from a small seed to a widely recognized name in the food industry.
